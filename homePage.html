ALL MAHARASHTRA THANG-TA ASSOCIATION

All Maharashtra Thang Ta Association is an state level body constituted duly registered under societies registration Act 1860 ( 21 of 1860 ) and P. T. Act 1950 (29 of 1950 ). The date of commencement of establishment of AMTTA , is 24th Nov. 1993 and had registered in the year 2009, having affiliated 36 Districts units in Maharashtra state for the purpose of Thang-Ta Martial Arts promotion.

Thang-Ta, known as HUYEN LALLONG, encompasses more than combat skills, emphasizing physical culture with breathing methods, meditations, and rituals. Some sword and spear forms are purely ritualistic, reserved for special occasions or circumstances. The core of Thang-Ta lies in sword training, with numerous drills for mastering basic strokes and footwork, including both two-person sets and solo practices.
